Where is Las Vegas?

The city of Las Vegas is in Nevada which is in the West of the United States. Las Vegas is a southern city in Nevada, the city is pretty much on the southern point of Nevada when looking at a map. Las Vegas is about 120 miles east from Death Valley National Park, about 68 miles north from Mojave National Preserve, and about 130 miles west from Grand Canyon-Parashant National Monument. Las Vegas is nearly next to Red Rock Canyon National Park which is about 19 minutes away.
